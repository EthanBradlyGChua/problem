The 4 main issues



1. Food insecurity affects millions globally due to poverty, conflict, and natural disasters,
making it challenging to ensure access to adequate and nutritious food.



2. Food production systems are strained by environmental factors like climate change,
soil degradation, and water scarcity, leading to reduced crop yields.



3. Poor infrastructure in developing regions makes it difficult for food to be transported
efficiently, causing significant food waste and unequal distribution.



4. Lack of education and awareness about sustainable agriculture
and nutrition perpetuates cycles of malnutrition and hunger.
